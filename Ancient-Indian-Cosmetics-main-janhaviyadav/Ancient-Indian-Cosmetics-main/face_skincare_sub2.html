Face and skincare

Lepa

  • Apparatus: Bowl, grinding stone (sil-batta), spoon
  • Procedure: Grind herbal powders → mix with oil/ghee/water → make thick paste → apply on affected area → leave for some time → wash off
  • Medical Uses: Treats acne, reduces inflammation, heals wounds, soothes skin
  • Sanskrit Reference: Sushruta Samhita

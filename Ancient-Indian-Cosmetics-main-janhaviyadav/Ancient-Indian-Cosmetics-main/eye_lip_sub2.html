Eye and lip adornment

Adhara Prasadhanam

  • Apparatus: Small bowl, spoon, heating vessel
  • Procedure: Melt beeswax and ghee → add herbal extracts/beetroot → mix well → cool to form balm → apply on lips
  • Medical Uses: Moisturizes lips, heals cracks, protects from dryness
  • Sanskrit Reference: Charaka Samhita

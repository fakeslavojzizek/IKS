Face and skincare

Ubtan

  • Apparatus: Bowl, spoon, grinding stone (sil-batta)
  • Procedure: Mix turmeric, gram flour, sandalwood with milk/rose water → make paste → apply → let it dry → wash off
  • Medical Uses: Cleans skin, removes tan, anti-bacterial, improves glow
  • Sanskrit Reference: Charaka Samhita

Hair Care and Styling

Oiling

  • Apparatus: Bowl, heating vessel, strainer, oil bottle
  • Procedure: Boil Bhringaraj leaves with coconut/sesame oil → cool → strain → store → massage onto scalp
  • Medical Uses: Promotes hair growth, reduces hair fall, prevents dandruff, nourishes scalp
  • Sanskrit Reference: Ashtanga Hridaya
